About Us

DisciplineMetrics was founded by a team of educators and data scientists passionate about creating better learning environments through data-driven insights.

Our mission is to help schools foster positive behavior by understanding patterns and implementing targeted interventions that work. We believe that by measuring and analyzing discipline metrics, we can help create more supportive and effective educational spaces.

With our platform, educators can track progress, identify areas for improvement, and celebrate successes in building a positive school culture.
